I'm Owen Laycock, and technology has been a lifelong fascination for me. Its impact extends not only to my personal journey but also to those within my sphere of influence. My primary pursuit involves unraveling the intricate web of the internet, a subject that never ceases to intrigue me.

Networking became a particular focus due to my avid interest in gaming, where the frustrations of network-related issues are all too familiar to me and others alike. I began exploring concepts like port forwarding, network security, and subnets well before my fourteenth birthday after being given a gaming computer with network issues. It's a bit of a mystery why I have such a strong affinity for technology, but it's something that comes naturally to me. Maybe these skill sets are from a past life or maybe they are skills acquired for a greater destiny.

While some might speculate that my loving family played a role in shaping my interests, I must clarify that none of my family members share my specific passion for technology. Nevertheless, I am convinced that my purpose lies in advancing technology and fostering connectivity among individuals through the internet and electronic devices. After having helped my grandpa understand how to use a modern phone and seeing him advance and connect ever so deep with it I have come to the conclusion that Technology is great! These tools possess a captivating quality that can not only bridge gaps in geographical distances but they also contain the ability to propel the human race eons forward.
